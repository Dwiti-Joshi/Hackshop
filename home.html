Welcome to hackshop!!

Here we provide all the information about hackathon and Aid those who are struggling with finding suitable problem statement as there is no such existing tool for this. Thus Our key feature is to suggest the idea to the user based on their skill we will take some user input about what skills they have and their abilities for domain And on that basis we will suggest them the suitable idea in which they can do well enough. In Addition to this we also update them for the upcoming hackathons and can also share their experience to guide other participants. User can access level 2 post hackathon by uploading their participation certificate where they can schedule the meeting with experts where they can get guidance about path after hackathon, how they can convert their hackathon idea to startup, which next technology they need to learn, etc.... So this is what our prototype is providing one platform for all.
